## Command to simulate a post so that a web front-end doesn't need to be developed yet.

curl --header "Content-Type: application/json"   --request POST   --data '{"username":"xyz","password":"xyz"}'   http://localhost:8080/add/user/account

curl --header "Content-Type: application/json"   --request POST   --data '{"username":"xyz","password":"xyz","first_name":"firstName","last_name":"lastName","email_address":"email@address.com","phone_number":"7031231234","birth_date":"01041940"}'   http://localhost:8080/add/user/account
